About Us

CareLink is a platform designed to help guardians manage the healthcare of their elderly loved ones, regardless of distance. From virtual appointments to medication tracking, CareLink makes healthcare easier for families.

Our mission is to provide seamless healthcare services to ensure the well-being of elderly individuals, even if their caregivers are far away.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

You can easily book an appointment with healthcare professionals through our scheduling system. Just select the time and date that works for you.

Is CareLink available 24/7?

Yes, our platform and customer support are available 24/7 to assist you at any time.

Can I track my loved one's medication?

Yes, CareLink offers medication tracking features to ensure your loved one's prescriptions are managed properly.
